Struggling with technology? Get free website help from Patch the Web.

Qualifications

I design free, simple websites for small businesses, nonprofits, and individuals that do not already have a website. I also offer free help to those who have a website already and meet one of the criteria below:

  • You have not received a website update in the last 5 years.
  • Your website has major accessibility issues.
  • Your website is not mobile-friendly.

Priority is given to residents of Virginia; however, I will consider remote requests from other locations if I have time.

Process

Registration

To get started, please fill out the Patch the Web form. I will review your request and contact you to discuss your needs. If you are eligible, I will work with you to create a simple website. The process typically takes a few weeks, depending on the complexity of your needs and my availability.

Materials

Please prepare any content you want on your website, such as text, images, logos, and color schemes. If you are not sure what to put on your website, I can help fill in the gaps.

Design

After we discuss the content, I will create prototype for your website. The design will work well on both desktop and mobile devices. It will also be accessible to users with disabilities.

Review

Before the website is published, we will review it and discuss any last-minute changes.

Launch

The website will be published on a free hosting service. I'll share the link with you, and I'll do my best to provide resources and support to help you maintain your website after it is live. However, I cannot guarantee ongoing support or maintenance.

Enjoy your new website!
